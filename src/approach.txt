TodoList(props, {value:'', todos: [{item:'Buy vegetables', hasDone: false}]})
  Todo({item, hasDone, onClick})

--------------------------------------------------
TodoList: 
  On changing input:
    - onChange handler will be called to update the value
  On pressing enter:
    - todo will be added to the list

Todo:
  On clicking on the todo item:
    - onClick received from props, will be called with the todoId 
    - toggles the hasDone field of the specific todo
